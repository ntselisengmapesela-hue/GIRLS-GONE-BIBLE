PERSONA

Ntseliseng Mapesela is the voice behind Girls Gone bible, a podcast dedicated to healing, Faith, and relatonships. A broadcasting and journalism major, Ntseliseng combines her passion for storytelling with her love for God, creating a safe and inspiring space where young women can navigate life's challenges with grace and faith. As a God-fearing individual, she values love that is guided by faith by faith, self-worth rooted in biblical truth, and personal growth through life experiences. She embraces a soft and feminine identity, often epressing interest in conversations that inspire healing and self empowerment

WHAT SHE IS KNOWN FOR

Known for her warm , approachable style, she gently guides listeners through topics like heartbreaks, self discovery, and spiritual growth, offering encouragement and biblical wisdom along the way. when she is not behind the microphone, Ntseliseng enjoys coffe dates, journaling, and celebrating the beauty of evryday life through a God centered-lens.

WHAT IS HER MISSION

Her mission is simple, to remind every listener that they are worthy, chosen, and becoming. She reminds women that thier worth is not defined by heartbreak, rejection, or life challenges, but by the love and purpose God has placed within them. She seeks to encourage her audience to trust God's timing, embrace personal growth, and develop healthy, meaningful relatiosnhips rooted in faith and respect. One episode at a time!
